Frequently Asked Questions

What is the approval workflow?

All events must be submitted by a Student Organizer, reviewed by a Faculty Head, and finally approved by the Department Head. You can track the progress in your dashboard.

How long does the approval take?

Typical turnaround time is 3-5 business days. You will receive an email notification once a decision is made at each stage.

Can I edit a submitted event?

You can only edit events that are in "Draft" status or if "Changes Requested" was initiated by a reviewer. Approved events cannot be edited.

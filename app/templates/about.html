About the System

The Automated Event Planner and Approval System is designed to streamline the complex process of organizing institutional events. By automating the workflow from proposal to final approval, we ensure efficiency, transparency, and accountability.

Key Objectives

  • Reduce manual paperwork and bureaucratic delays.
  • Ensure all prerequisite approvals are collected systematically.
  • Provide a centralized dashboard for all event-related activities.
